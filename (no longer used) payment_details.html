    CONFIRMATION OF REGISTRATION

    Confirmation of registration is only upon full payment of the registration fee. ICDXA committee will
    notify the registrants on the registration confirmation by email according to details provided in the
    registration form. If you do not receive the registration confirmation two weeks after submitting your
    registration form and payment, please contact ICDXA.

    CANCELLATION POLICY

    There is no refund for cancellation of registration. If you are unable to attend the symposium, please
    submit the replacement name and contact details in writing via email to ICDXA.

    DISCLAIMER

    TAR University College reserves the right to amend or change the program, venue or speakers, or cancel
    the symposium. In the event of cancellation, all registration fees paid will be refunded. No further claims
    shall be entertained.
