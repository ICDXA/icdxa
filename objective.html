    Conference Objectives

    The objective of the International Conference on Digital Transformation and Applications (ICDXA 2021) is to promote research, education, and industry implementation that involves Digital transformation (DX) processes from different fields of interests such as big data analytics, Industry 4.0, computational intelligence, deep learning, computer vision, robotics, human computer interaction, communication, networking, security, technology applications in scientific, engineering, and social sciences.

    ICDXA 2021

    The 2nd International Conference on Digital Transformation and Applications brings you a unique experience with the speeches from renowned speakers, best presentations, roundtable discussions, networking opportunities and many more.
